Reachability

The Ziggo Dome is easily accessible! Whether you come by car, bicycle or public transport: we are unmissable. The Ziggo Dome is ideally located next to the highway, near the train station + metro and bus stop Amsterdam Bijlmer ArenA.

About us

Sustainability

You will experience unforgettable evenings in the Ziggo Dome! There are all kinds of things involved. Sustainability is our top priority and we do everything we can to make the Ziggo Dome greener.

History

Before Beyoncé, Lady Gaga, Prince, Pearl Jam, and Armin van Buuren graced the backstage halls and stage of the Ziggo Dome, there was... that's right: a cigar box. And a dream. That's where it all began. We would love to take you on a journey through the timeline of the Ziggo Dome.
